Financial Institutions

How do you manage your money as you grow? Financial Institutions!

Financial institutions" might sound like a big word to you now, but it just refers to places that help people with their money.

These places help people save, borrow, and even invest their money to make it grow! Just like you keep your toys in a safe spot, adults keep their money safe in these financial institutions.

Did you know: Banks are one type of financial institution. There are also credit unions and investment companies.
